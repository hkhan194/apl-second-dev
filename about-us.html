About APL

The APL is an association of more than 1100 lawyers who practise pension law in the UK. Our members include most, if not all, of the leading practitioners in the field. The APL began life in 1984, principally as a forum for exchanging ideas amongst lawyers advising in a relatively new legal specialism. As we have grown as a professional body, we have extended our range of activities.
